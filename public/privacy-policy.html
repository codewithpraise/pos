Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Valenixia ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Valenixia POS software, mobile application, and related services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our Service. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide and improve the Service:

  • Account Information: Business name, email address, and phone number provided during registration. Passwords are stored as salted, one-way cryptographic hashes — we cannot recover your password.
  • Transaction Data: Sales records, inventory entries, receipt data, and any other business data you enter into the Service. This data belongs to you.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, hardware identifier, and application version, collected for licensing verification and crash diagnostics.
  • Crash and Diagnostic Logs: Anonymised error reports to help us identify and fix software defects. These do not include financial transaction data.
  • Payment Reference Information: Transaction reference numbers (RRN) from NayaPay, Raast, EasyPaisa, or SadaPay submitted for subscription payment verification. We do not store full account numbers, card details, or bank credentials.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Service;
  • To verify your license and enforce subscription terms;
  • To generate receipts and process transactions as directed by you;
  • To comply with legal obligations, including transmission of invoice data to the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) if you are a registered taxpayer and have enabled FBR integration;
  • To send service-related communications (not marketing, unless you explicitly opt in);
  • To investigate fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.

4. FBR Data Reporting

If you have enabled FBR integration and are a registered taxpayer in Pakistan, your transaction and invoice data will be transmitted to the Federal Board of Revenue via the FBR's official API in accordance with applicable Pakistani tax law. You are responsible for the accuracy of data submitted to FBR through our Service. Valenixia is not responsible for FBR penalties, rejections, or disputes arising from incorrect data you provide.

5. Data Storage and Security

Transaction data is stored locally on your device first (local-first architecture). When cloud synchronisation is enabled, data is replicated to secure cloud servers. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We will notify you of any data breach that materially affects your information as soon as reasonably practicable.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors necessary to operate the Service (e.g., cloud hosting, Supabase for database synchronisation). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by a valid order of a Pakistani court or competent regulatory authority, including under the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016, or any other applicable Pakistani law.
  • FBR Reporting: Invoice and transaction data transmitted to FBR where legally required, as described in Section 4.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, your data may be transferred. We will provide notice before such transfer.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable Pakistani law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to any legal retention obligations;
  • Restrict or object to certain processing of your data where applicable law permits;
  • Receive a copy of your data in a commonly used format (data portability).

To exercise any of these rights, email legal@valenixia.com. We will respond within thirty (30) calendar days. We may require identity verification before processing your request.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymise your personal data within ninety (90) calendar days, except where retention is required by law. Transaction records may be retained for up to six (6) years to comply with Pakistani tax record-keeping requirements applicable to businesses registered with FBR.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

We use session tokens and local storage mechanisms (IndexedDB, localStorage) solely for application functionality, such as maintaining your login session, storing offline transaction data, and remembering your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising trackers, analytics platforms, or behavioural profiling cookies.

10. International Data Transfers

Cloud synchronisation may result in your data being stored on servers located outside Pakistan. By enabling cloud synchronisation, you consent to this transfer. We use service providers that maintain appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. If you require data to remain within Pakistan, you may operate the Service in offline-only mode.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for use by businesses and is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notice at least fourteen (14) days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

13. Contact

For privacy-related questions, requests, or complaints, contact: legal@valenixia.com

We aim to respond to all enquiries within thirty (30) calendar days.
