Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 13, 2026

Sole Proprietor Disclosure: Valenixia is operated by Muhammad Soban Ali as a sole proprietorship based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. No corporate entity is involved in the operation of this service.

1. Information We Collect

Valenixia POS operates entirely on your local device and your self-hosted server. We do not operate cloud servers that collect your data. The following data is stored:

  • Business Data: Products, inventory, transactions, customer records, and sales history — stored locally in your browser (IndexedDB) and on your server (SQLite).
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and browser type for compatibility purposes.
  • License Information: A hardware-derived ID (HWID) and license tier for authentication.
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous error logs stored locally for debugging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide POS functionality (sales processing, inventory management, reporting)
  • To validate your software license
  • To improve app stability through anonymous crash reports
  • To sync data between your devices (only if you explicitly enable sync)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage & Security

All business data is stored:

  • Locally on device using IndexedDB (encrypted at rest by the browser)
  • On your server using SQLite (you control server security)
  • Optional cloud backup to your own Supabase/Google Drive account only

You own your data. You can export or delete it at any time through the Settings menu.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage for:

  • Authentication tokens (device pairing)
  • User preferences (theme, language)
  • Form drafts (auto-save)

No tracking cookies. No third-party analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

Valenixia POS may connect to:

  • Your self-hosted server (required for multi-device sync)
  • Supabase (only if you enable cloud backup)
  • Google Drive (only if you enable backup to Drive)
  • WhatsApp (only when you explicitly share receipts)

Each third-party connection requires your explicit authorization.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete all local data by clearing browser storage or using the "Reset Application" option in Settings. Server data is retained until you delete the SQLite database file.

7. Children's Privacy

Valenixia POS is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / UAE PDPL)

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.
